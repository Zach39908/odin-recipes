Crepes

Description

This list will inform you of everything you need to make a batch of fresh crepe batter, using common household ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Begin whisking the flour and eggs. Steadily add in the milk and water while stirring. Add in the butter and salt and beat until smooth.
  2. Taking a griddle or frying pan with light cooking oil, place over a medium high heat stove. Pour the batter onto the griddle with around 1/4 cup for each crepe. Tilt the pan in a circular pattern to spread the batter across the surface.
  3. Cook one crepe for 2 minutes or until lightly brown on the bottom. Flip with a spatula to other side and do the same. Then take it off serve!
